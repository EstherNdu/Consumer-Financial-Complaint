# Consumer-Financial-Complaint

# Brief Introduction
This report delves into customer complaints across 1,081 companies in all 51 U.S. states for Onyx Data Challenge. This report enables in-depth analysis of complaint trends, product and sub-product issues, response timeliness, and company behavior across regions. It demonstrates how large-scale consumer data can be transformed into actionable insights that support regulatory oversight, customer experience evaluation, and strategic decision-making when effectively modeled and visualized. It also highlights Key Performer Indicators such as Total complaints, average timely response, average reputation score and average market share. 

# About The Dataset
This project uses a two-table dataset consisting of 62,516 consumer complaint records and 1,081 company records. The data captures detailed information on financial products, complaint issues, company responses, and resolution timelines. It provides a solid foundation for analyzing complaint patterns, response effectiveness, and company-level behavior.

# Problem Questions
* What is the trend in complaints over the years
* Which channel had the most complaints and how long did it rake to respond via these channels
* Which products had highest complaint share
* Which states had the highest complaint share
* How did complaints vary across states and region
* Which company tier had the highest reputation score
* Do companies with higher market share have high reputation score?
